H&J's DarkRP Rules

All rules are strictly enforced, each rule broken will be added to your permanent record. Please note that the server is still in development and that things are likely to change.
  1. You don't need to advert to do anything like raiding.
  2. No Mass or random killing (MassDM). This means do not kill everyone you see because you can. Killing people because you don't like them isn't tolerated.
  3. Do not prop surf, prop kill or prop climb. This along with trolling is not tolerated.
  4. Do not use scripts or exploits.
  5. If your base is being raided and you die, you're free to return to the base as many times as you'd like in an attempt to repell the attackers.
  6. When you raid someone else's base and you die, you're not allowed to repeatedly return to their base to continue raiding.
  7. While murder and mugging is allowed, constantly returning to someone to kill them is not tolerated and is classed as MassDM.
  8. While NLR, FearRP and FailRP are not rules on this server it is encouraged to use them as guidelines for roleplaying. For further explanation, Read this.
  1. Keypads must have a timer of 3 seconds minimum.
  2. No more than 3 fading doors in a row to enter a base.
  3. A base MUST be raidable.
  4. You may not raid bases with 'Building' signs outside
  5. You're allowed to put up a 'Building' sign while building, but you may not have any items within your base including Printers, Bitminers, drugs or shipments of any kind.
  1. You are allowed to be a gun dealer and spawn your self weapons to use, and for your friends however it is advisable to use the item store system to store weapons you buy in banks located around the map.
  2. As any government jobs you are allowed to be corrupt and use money printers and other such illegal items. Note however other government jobs are allowed to demote and ban you from said jobs for breaking the law.
  3. The bank is only robbable when there are atleast 2 government jobs or bank roles active.
